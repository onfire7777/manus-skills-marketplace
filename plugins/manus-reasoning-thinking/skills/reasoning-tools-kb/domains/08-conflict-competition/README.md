# Conflict & Competition

Reasoning tools for strategic interaction and adversarial situations.

## Domains in This Category

1. [Military Strategy](military-strategy.md) - Maneuver warfare, centers of gravity, fog of war, strategic surprise
2. [Competitive Game Theory](game-theory-competitive.md) - Nash equilibrium, mixed strategies, backward induction
3. [Litigation Strategy](litigation-strategy.md) - Burden of proof, discovery, precedent, adversarial process

## Common Themes

- Anticipating opponent moves and counter-moves
- Managing uncertainty and incomplete information
- Creating and exploiting asymmetries
- Choosing when to compete vs cooperate
- Understanding strategic commitment and credibility

## Cross-Domain Tools

Tools that appear across multiple domains in this category:
- Game trees and backward induction
- Mixed strategies and unpredictability
- Commitment and credible threats
- Information asymmetry exploitation
- Focal points and coordination
