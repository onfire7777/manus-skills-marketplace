# Privacy Maturity Model Workflows

## Workflow 1: Annual Maturity Assessment

1. **Planning** (Week 1): Define scope, schedule domain workshops, distribute pre-assessment questionnaires
2. **Domain Workshops** (Weeks 2-4): Conduct 90-minute assessment workshops for each of 10 domains
3. **Scoring** (Week 5): Calculate domain scores, overall weighted score, and maturity rating
4. **Visualization** (Week 5): Generate radar chart and maturity profile
5. **Gap Analysis** (Week 6): Compare current scores against targets; prioritize gaps
6. **Roadmap** (Weeks 6-7): Develop phased improvement roadmap
7. **Benchmarking** (Week 7): Compare against industry peers and prior year
8. **Reporting** (Week 8): Present to executive leadership and board
9. **Target Setting** (Week 8): Set maturity targets for the following year

## Workflow 2: Domain Assessment Workshop

1. **Context Setting** (10 min): Review domain scope and maturity level definitions
2. **Current State Presentation** (30 min): Domain owner presents capabilities, evidence, and challenges
3. **Evidence Review** (20 min): Assessor reviews documentation and asks clarifying questions
4. **Maturity Rating** (20 min): Collaborative rating using criteria; build consensus on score
5. **Gap Discussion** (10 min): Identify priority gaps and potential quick wins

## Workflow 3: Roadmap Execution and Tracking

1. **Initiative Planning**: Break each roadmap phase into specific initiatives with milestones
2. **Resource Allocation**: Assign owners, budget, and timelines for each initiative
3. **Quarterly Progress Review**: Track completion against roadmap milestones
4. **Mid-Year Check**: Reassess progress; adjust roadmap if needed
5. **Year-End Assessment**: Conduct full maturity reassessment to measure improvement
6. **Board Reporting**: Report maturity progression and next-year targets to board
