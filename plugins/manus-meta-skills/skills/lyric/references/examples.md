## Example 1: Indie Pop (English)

### Style Prompt
```
indie pop, female vocal, breathy, mid-tempo, acoustic guitar, piano, nostalgic, polished production
```

### Lyrics
```
[Short Instrumental Intro]

[Verse 1]
Walking through the rain at 3 AM
Your name still glowing on my phone
I can't stop reading what you said
Every word cuts straight to bone

[Pre-Chorus]
Maybe I should let it go
Maybe I already know

[Chorus]
But I keep coming back to you (ooh)
Like the tide keeps finding shore
Every time I think we're through
I just want you even more

[Verse 2]
Coffee's getting cold again
Your jacket's hanging by the door
I pretend I don't remember when
You said forever, meant it more

[Pre-Chorus]
Maybe I should let it go
Maybe I already know

[Chorus]
But I keep coming back to you (ooh)
Like the tide keeps finding shore
Every time I think we're through
I just want you even more

[Bridge | soft | piano only]
What if I just stayed this time
What if you let down your guard

[Final Chorus | high energy | full mix]
But I keep coming back to you (ooh)
Like the tide keeps finding shore
Every time I think we're through
I just want you even more

[Outro]
[Fade Out]
```

### Notes
- 文字数: ~820 / 3,000
- 行数: 30
- 構成: Intro → V1 → PC → C → V2 → PC → C → Bridge → FC → Outro
- 技法: 具体的描写（3 AM, coffee, jacket）、タイドのメタファー、(ooh)アドリブ
- 推奨生成回数: 4-6回
