# NIST PF GOVERN Function — Implementation Workflows

## Workflow 1: Privacy Governance Structure Establishment

### Steps
1. **Executive Sponsorship**: Secure C-suite commitment to privacy governance
2. **Role Definition**: Define CPO/DPO, privacy team, and privacy champion roles
3. **RACI Development**: Create RACI matrices for all privacy functions
4. **Reporting Lines**: Establish reporting structure to board/executive committee
5. **Charter Creation**: Draft privacy committee charter with authority and scope
6. **Budget Allocation**: Secure annual privacy program budget
7. **Tool Selection**: Evaluate and procure privacy management tools

## Workflow 2: Privacy Training Program Deployment

### Steps
1. **Needs Assessment**: Identify training requirements by role and department
2. **Curriculum Design**: Develop tiered training content (general, role-specific, specialist)
3. **Platform Setup**: Configure LMS with privacy training modules
4. **Delivery Schedule**: Set annual training calendar with deadlines
5. **Assessment Design**: Create quizzes and scenario-based evaluations
6. **Tracking and Reporting**: Monitor completion rates and report to governance
7. **Continuous Update**: Refresh content based on regulatory changes and incidents

## Workflow 3: Privacy Policy Lifecycle Management

### Steps
1. **Inventory Existing Policies**: Catalog all privacy-related policies
2. **Gap Analysis**: Identify missing policies against regulatory requirements
3. **Drafting**: Write new policies with stakeholder input
4. **Legal Review**: Ensure legal compliance across jurisdictions
5. **Approval**: Route through governance approval workflow
6. **Publication**: Distribute to relevant audiences
7. **Acknowledgment**: Track employee policy acknowledgment
8. **Review Cycle**: Schedule annual review with version control

## Workflow 4: Privacy Monitoring and Review

### Steps
1. **Define KPIs**: Establish privacy performance indicators
2. **Data Collection**: Configure automated metric collection
3. **Dashboard Setup**: Build privacy metrics dashboards
4. **Review Cadence**: Set quarterly operational and annual strategic reviews
5. **Findings Integration**: Feed review outcomes into improvement plans
6. **Stakeholder Reporting**: Present findings to governance bodies
7. **Benchmarking**: Compare performance against industry peers
